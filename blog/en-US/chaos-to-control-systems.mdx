## The Myth of "Expensive Software"

In the past, having your own system required buying physical servers, paying for expensive licenses, and hiring a huge team. Today, the game has changed.

I use a modern, "Serverless" architecture. This means you **only pay for what you use**. If the system is accessed by 10 people, you pay pennies. If it scales to 10,000, the infrastructure grows automatically with it.

To illustrate, let's look at **Kingfix**, a system I developed focused on management and services.

Instead of hiring an entire agency, the client relied on my Fullstack expertise to deliver the solution end-to-end:

1.  **Database (Neon DB):** High speed and security for customer data.
2.  **Authentication (Clerk):** Secure login (Google, Email), just like major banks.
3.  **Payments (Stripe):** Automatic financial integration. Money lands in the account without manual checking.
4.  **Interface (Next.js 15 + Tailwind):** A beautiful, fast admin dashboard that works perfectly on mobile.

### The Result?

A system with an "enterprise feel" but with **negligible monthly fixed costs**. The client has total control of the operation in the palm of their hand, and the system became a company **asset** (something that increases the business's market value).
